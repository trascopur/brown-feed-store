Our Story

Founded in 1985 by the Brown family, our feed store has been the cornerstone of agricultural supply in Lampasas County. What started as a small family operation has grown into a trusted resource for farmers, ranchers, and pet owners throughout Central Texas. We believe in supporting our local community with quality products, fair prices, and the kind of personal service that only comes from knowing our customers and their unique needs.
